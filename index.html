Frequently Asked Questions

Is my password stored anywhere?
No! Your real password is only stored locally on YOUR device using your browser's encrypted storage. It never leaves your computer and is never sent to any server.
Does it work on all websites?
Yes! The extension works on any website with a password field. It automatically detects login forms and protects them.
What if I forget the typing order?
The extension shows you the order every time you focus on the password field. You can also view saved instructions in the extension popup.
Is this really free?
Absolutely! Created by Mugisha Billy Chriss, this extension is completely free and open source. No premium features, no subscriptions, ever.
